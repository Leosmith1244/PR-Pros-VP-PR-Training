VPPR Quick Reference Cheat Sheet

"You make the value of your club visible before the meeting and memorable after it."

The VPPR Mindset

  • You are a storyteller, not a salesperson.
  • Consistency beats creativity.
  • Authentic beats perfect.
  • Visibility creates opportunity.

Core Responsibilities

Every Month: Promote meetings, Share 1 win, Coordinate follow-up.

Every Term: Support campaigns, Strengthen online brand, Collaborate with officers.

The Story Formula

Person → Challenge → Growth → Invitation

Example: "John joined nervous. Last week he gave his Ice Breaker. Join us to start your journey."

Simple Social System

  1. Faces: Members, speakers, officers.
  2. Wins: Awards, milestones.
  3. Invitations: Meetings, open houses.

Rhythm: 2 posts/week. Same content, different platforms.

The Guest Journey

1. Sees Post 2. Attends 3. Welcomed 4. Follow-up
